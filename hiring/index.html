    • Why Work for a Startup?

    Working for a startup has its challenges, such as sometimes irregular workloads, but we think the benefits are much greater! For one, you'll be part of a small team, which means no corporate bullshit. You'll also be playing a central role in shaping the future of this company. However, if you don't like where the company is headed, working for a startup opens many doors. Finally, IT'S FUN! Startups are exciting and unpredictable, and although this means that you may have to take on additional responsibilities on occasion, it also means you'll never get bored.
